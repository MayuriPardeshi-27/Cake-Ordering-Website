About Pink Petal Bakes ❤️

Pink Petal Bakes started with a simple love for baking and making people smile. What began as a small passion slowly turned into a dream of creating beautiful cakes for life’s most special moments.

We create fresh and custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and every celebration that deserves something sweet. Each cake is made with care, quality ingredients, and thoughtful designs that perfectly match your occasion.

For us, a cake is more than dessert. It becomes part of your memories. That is why we put our heart into every order, making sure it looks beautiful and tastes just as special.

Our Promise

To create cakes that bring joy, celebrate love, and make every moment unforgettable.
